<p><strong>The Secondary Encoders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absolute Encoders</li><li>Incremental Encoders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The secondary encoders market comprises two primary types: absolute encoders and incremental encoders. Absolute encoders provide a unique position value for each shaft angle, ensuring precise location tracking even after power loss. In contrast, incremental encoders track movement by generating pulses as the shaft rotates, requiring a reference point upon power restoration. Both types are essential in industrial automation, robotics, and manufacturing, offering solutions for position measurement and feedback in various applications, thereby driving market growth.</p></p>
