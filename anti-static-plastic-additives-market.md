<p><p>The Anti-Static Plastic Additives market is categorized into two types: Coating Type and Internal Additive Type. Coating Type additives are applied on the surface of plastic materials to prevent static electricity buildup. Internal Additive Type additives are incorporated into the plastic material during manufacturing to provide anti-static properties throughout the product. Both types of additives play a crucial role in reducing static electricity and improving the overall performance of plastic products in various industries.</p></p>
