<p><p>AMOLED (Active Matrix Organic Light Emitting Diode) panels are categorized into two main market types: flexible and rigid. Flexible AMOLED panels are lightweight and can be bent or curved, making them ideal for applications in wearables, smartphones, and innovative display designs. Rigid AMOLED panels, on the other hand, offer durability and are typically used in traditional display formats like televisions, monitors, and laptops. Both types benefit from high contrast ratios and vibrant colors, catering to diverse consumer and industrial needs.</p></p>
