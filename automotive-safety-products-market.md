<p><p>The automotive safety products market is divided into two main types: active and passive safety systems. Active safety products include technologies that prevent accidents before they occur, such as anti-lock braking systems, electronic stability control, and advanced driver-assistance systems. Passive safety products focus on protecting occupants during a collision, including airbags, seatbelts, and crumple zones. Together, these systems enhance vehicle safety by reducing the likelihood of accidents and minimizing injuries during crashes.</p></p>
