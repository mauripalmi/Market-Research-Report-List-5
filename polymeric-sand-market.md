<p><p>The polymeric sand market primarily consists of two key types: polymeric sand and polymeric dust. Polymeric sand is a specialized blend of sand and additives that harden when activated with water, used to fill joints between pavers, enhancing stability and preventing weed growth. Polymeric dust, often finer in texture, serves to fill smaller gaps and provide a smooth finish to paver surfaces. Both products are integral in landscaping and hardscaping projects, ensuring durability and longevity of paved areas.</p></p>
