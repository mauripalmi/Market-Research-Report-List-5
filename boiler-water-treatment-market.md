<p><ul><li>Corrosion Inhibitors</li><li>Scale Inhibitors</li><li>Oxygen Scavengers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Boiler water treatment market consists of corrosion inhibitors, scale inhibitors, and oxygen scavengers. Corrosion inhibitors are used to protect the boiler system from metal corrosion caused by dissolved oxygen and harmful chemicals. Scale inhibitors prevent the accumulation of scale deposits on the boiler surfaces, which can reduce efficiency and lead to equipment failure. Oxygen scavengers help remove dissolved oxygen from the water, thus preventing corrosion and prolonging the lifespan of the boiler system.</p></p>
