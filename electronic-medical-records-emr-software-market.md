<p><p>The Electronic Medical Records (EMR) software market is primarily categorized into cloud-based and web-based solutions. In contrast, web-based EMR software operates through internet browsers, allowing healthcare providers to access records without installing software locally. Both types enhance operational efficiency, improve patient care, and facilitate real-time data sharing, yet differ in infrastructure and management requirements.</p></p>
