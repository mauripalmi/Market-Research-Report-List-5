<p><p>Continuous Glucose Monitoring (CGM) systems for the elderly consist of two main market types: wearable and implantable devices. Wearable CGMs are easy to use, typically coming as patches or sensors applied to the skin, providing real-time glucose data through a mobile app. In contrast, implantable CGMs are surgically inserted under the skin for longer-term monitoring, offering improved accuracy and convenience. Both types aim to enhance diabetes management and improve health outcomes for the elderly population by promoting proactive care.</p></p>
