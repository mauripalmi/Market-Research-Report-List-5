<p><p>The Pleated Polyester Filter Cartridge market includes various types such as PP (Polypropylene), PES (Polyester), PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene), and Nylon filters. PP filters are known for their chemical resistance and durability, ideal for water and air filtration. PES filters offer high flow rates and fine particle retention, suitable for pharmaceutical and food industries. PTFE filters excel in high-temperature and chemical resistance applications. Nylon filters are valued for their strength and compatibility with solvents, commonly used in industrial and laboratory settings. Each type caters to specific filtration needs.</p></p>
