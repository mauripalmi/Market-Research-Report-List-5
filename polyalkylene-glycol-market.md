<p><p>The polyalkylene glycol market is primarily segmented into two types: polypropylene glycol (PPG) and polyethylene glycol (PEG). PPG is commonly used as an industrial lubricant, surfactant, and in the formulation of various plasticizers and foams, known for its hydrophobic properties. Conversely, PEG is hydrophilic, widely utilized in pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, and food processing due to its solubility and non-toxicity. Both types find applications across diverse industries, driving growth in demand for specialty chemicals and enhancing product performance.</p></p>
