<p><p>Maritime fenders are critical components used to absorb the kinetic energy during ship berthing, preventing damage to vessels and docking structures. Solid rubber fenders are robust and durable, offering high energy absorption and minimal maintenance. Pneumatic fenders are inflatable, providing lightweight versatility and adaptability to various vessel sizes. Foam fenders are filled with a closed-cell foam, offering buoyancy and excellent performance in rough seas. Each type serves unique applications based on energy absorption needs and environmental conditions.</p></p>
