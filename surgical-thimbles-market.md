Nitrile surgical thimbles are favored for their puncture resistance, durability, and hypoallergenic properties, making them suitable for a wider range of users, including those with latex allergies. Both types cater to distinct user preferences, ensuring safety and effectiveness in various surgical procedures.</p></p>
