<p><strong>The Thrombosis Viscoelastic Analysis System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thromboelastography (TEG)</li><li>Rotational thromboelastometry (ROTEM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thrombosis viscoelastic analysis system market primarily focuses on two main technologies: Thromboelastography (TEG) and Rotational thromboelastometry (ROTEM). TEG evaluates coagulation dynamics by measuring changes in blood viscosity over time, providing insights into clot formation and dissolution. ROTEM, on the other hand, uses a rotational approach to assess the viscoelastic properties of blood, enabling real-time monitoring of hemostasis. Both methods are crucial for guiding transfusion strategies and managing bleeding risks in various clinical settings.</p></p>
