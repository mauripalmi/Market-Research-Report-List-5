<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Gadolinium-based Contrast Agent Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear non-ionic agent</li><li>Linear ionic</li><li>Macrocyclic non-ionic</li><li>Macrocyclic ionic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gadolinium-based contrast agents (GBCAs) are categorized into four types based on their molecular structure and charge. Linear non-ionic agents have an open-chain structure without a net charge, offering lower osmolality. Linear ionic agents are open-chain with a negative charge, providing high stability but higher osmolality. Macrocyclic non-ionic agents have a ring-like structure without a charge, ensuring high stability and safety. Macrocyclic ionic agents feature a ring structure with a charge, combining stability with strong binding to gadolinium, reducing toxicity risks. Each type serves specific diagnostic imaging needs.</p></p>
