<p><p>High power reed switches above 30W are specialized components used in various applications, categorized mainly into Normally Open (NO) and Normally Closed (NC) types. The Normally Open type remains open until activated, allowing current to flow only when a magnet is nearby, making it ideal for safety applications and automation systems. Conversely, the Normally Closed type remains closed until activated, cutting off current when a magnet is introduced, suitable for fail-safe operations. These types cater to diverse industrial sectors needing reliable switching solutions.</p></p>
