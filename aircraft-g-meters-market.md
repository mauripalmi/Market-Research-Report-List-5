<p><p>Aircraft G-meters are used to measure the acceleration forces experienced during flight. There are three main types of G-meters: Piezoelectric Type, Piezoresistive Type, and Potentiometer Type. Piezoelectric G-meters use crystals to generate an electric charge in response to acceleration. Piezoresistive G-meters use semiconductor materials that change resistance under acceleration. Potentiometer G-meters use a sliding contact that moves along a resistor to measure acceleration. Each type has its own advantages and is used in various aircraft applications.</p></p>
