<p><p>The diaphragm valve market comprises three main types: stainless steel, cast iron, and plastic diaphragm valves. Stainless steel diaphragm valves are highly durable, corrosion-resistant, and suitable for high-pressure and high-temperature applications. Cast iron diaphragm valves offer strength and affordability, making them ideal for various industrial applications, particularly in water and wastewater management. Plastic diaphragm valves are lightweight, resistant to chemical corrosion, and suitable for low-pressure applications, commonly used in industries like pharmaceuticals and food processing.</p></p>
