<p><p>The pigmented paper market is classified into four main types based on the origin of pigments. Organic pigments, derived from carbon-based compounds, are known for their vibrant colors and good transparency. Synthetic organic pigments, created through chemical processes, offer enhanced stability and a wide color range. Inorganic pigments, sourced from natural minerals, provide durability and opacity. Finally, synthetic inorganic pigments are engineered for specific applications, combining benefits of both organic and inorganic options, catering to diverse requirements in the paper industry.</p></p>
