<p><p>The semiconductor chemical vapor deposition (CVD) equipment market is categorized into three main types: Atmospheric Pressure CVD (APCVD), Low-Pressure CVD (LPCVD), and Ultrahigh Vacuum CVD (UHV CVD). APCVD operates at atmospheric pressure, allowing for faster deposition rates and simpler setup; LPCVD significantly reduces pressure to enhance film uniformity and quality, making it suitable for advanced applications; UHV CVD utilizes extremely low pressures to minimize contamination and enable high-quality deposition for critical semiconductor devices.</p></p>
