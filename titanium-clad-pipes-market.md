<p><strong>The Titanium Clad Pipes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Roll Bonding Process</li><li>Mechanically</li><li>Explosive Bonding Process</li><li>Weld Overlay Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Titanium clad pipes are manufactured through various methods, each offering unique benefits for different applications. The roll bonding process involves pressing titanium sheets onto a substrate, creating a strong bond through heat and pressure. Mechanically bonded pipes utilize mechanical interlocking for adhesion. Explosive bonding employs controlled explosive charges to achieve a metallurgical bond, while weld overlay involves fusing titanium to the surface of a pipe using welding techniques. Each method enhances corrosion resistance and durability, catering to specific industrial needs.</p></p>
