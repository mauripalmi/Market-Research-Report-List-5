<p><p>Healthcare Integration Engines (HIEs) facilitate the transfer and management of health information across different systems. The market is divided into two main types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise HIEs are hosted locally within healthcare facilities, providing control over data security and compliance but requiring significant upfront investment. Cloud-based HIEs, conversely, are hosted remotely, offering scalability, lower initial costs, and easier access across multiple locations. Both types aim to enhance interoperability and streamline healthcare workflows.</p></p>
