<p><p>The CCTV video camera market consists of three main types: Analog Cameras, IP Cameras, and Hybrid Cameras. Analog cameras transmit video signals using coaxial cables, providing basic surveillance with lower costs. IP cameras use digital technology, offering higher resolution, remote access, and advanced features like motion detection. Hybrid cameras combine both analog and IP functionalities, allowing users to integrate existing analog systems with newer IP technology, providing flexibility and scalability for varying surveillance needs.</p></p>
