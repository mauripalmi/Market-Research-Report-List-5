<p><p>The liquid phenoxy resins market is primarily divided into two types: waterborne phenoxy resins and solventborne phenoxy resins. Waterborne phenoxy resins are environmentally friendly, offering reduced volatile organic compound (VOC) emissions, making them suitable for sustainable applications. In contrast, solventborne phenoxy resins are characterized by their higher performance in terms of chemical resistance and physical properties. Both types find applications in coatings, adhesives, and other industrial products, catering to diverse customer needs and regulatory requirements.</p></p>
