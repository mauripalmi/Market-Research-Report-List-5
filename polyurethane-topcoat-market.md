<p><strong>The Polyurethane Topcoat Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-based Topcoat</li><li>Oil-based Topcoat</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyurethane topcoat market includes water-based and oil-based types. Water-based topcoats use water as a solvent, offering eco-friendliness, low VOC emissions, quick drying, and easy cleanup. They are ideal for indoor applications and environments prioritizing sustainability. Oil-based topcoats use organic solvents, providing superior durability, high gloss, and resistance to wear, making them suitable for high-traffic areas and outdoor use. However, they emit higher VOCs and require longer drying times. Both types cater to diverse needs, balancing performance, environmental impact, and application requirements.</p></p>
