<p><p>The Machine Room Less (MRL) passenger elevator market primarily includes two types: traction and hydraulic elevators. Traction MRL elevators utilize a system of ropes and pulleys, offering energy efficiency and smoother rides, making them ideal for mid to high-rise buildings. In contrast, hydraulic MRL elevators operate using a hydraulic pump to lift the cabin, providing a compact design suitable for low-rise applications. Both types enhance space utilization and reduce installation costs while maintaining safety and performance standards.</p></p>
