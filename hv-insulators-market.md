<p><p>The HV insulator market comprises three main types: porcelain, glass, and composite insulators. Porcelain insulators are known for their durability and high mechanical strength, making them ideal for long-term installations. Glass insulators offer excellent insulation properties and visibility for damage assessment, though they can be heavier. Composite insulators, made from polymer materials, provide lightweight and flexible alternatives with good resistance to environmental factors. Each type has specific advantages, catering to different application needs in high-voltage transmission systems.</p></p>
