Polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE) is renowned for its non-stick properties and broad usage in cookware and gaskets. Lastly, Polyethylenetetrafluoroethylene (ETFE) is recognized for its durability and is widely applied in architectural films and coatings. These materials excel across diverse industries due to their unique characteristics.</p></p>
