<p><ul><li>Photoelectric</li><li>Dual Sensors</li><li>Ionization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The residential smart smoke detectors market features three main types: photoelectric, dual sensor, and ionization detectors. Photoelectric detectors utilize a light source to sense smoke particles, making them effective for smoldering fires. Ionization detectors contain radioactive material that detects fast-flaming fires by measuring ion flow changes. Dual sensor detectors combine both photoelectric and ionization technologies, offering comprehensive protection against various fire types. This diversity allows consumers to choose detectors that best match their specific safety needs and home environments.</p></p>
