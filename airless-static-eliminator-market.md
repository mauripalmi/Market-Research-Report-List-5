<p><p>The Airless Static Eliminator market is categorized mainly into two types: Bar Type and Spot Type. Bar Type static eliminators span longer surfaces, providing uniform discharge across an area, making them suitable for large-scale applications like conveyors and manufacturing lines. In contrast, Spot Type eliminators are designed for targeted areas, effectively neutralizing static at specific points, ideal for smaller parts or delicate processes. Both types enhance product quality by eliminating static electricity without the use of compressed air.</p></p>
