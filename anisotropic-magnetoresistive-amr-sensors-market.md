<p><p>Anisotropic Magnetoresistive (AMR) sensors are advanced devices that exploit the magnetoresistive effect to detect magnetic fields and variations in magnetic flux. These sensors are characterized by their ability to exhibit a change in electrical resistance in response to an external magnetic field, making them ideal for applications such as automotive positioning, industrial equipment, and consumer electronics.
