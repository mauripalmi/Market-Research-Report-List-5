<p><p>The steering systems market comprises three main types: Electronic Power Steering (EPS), which uses electric motors to assist steering, enhancing fuel efficiency and reducing weight; Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS), which combines hydraulic assistance with electric control for improved responsiveness and efficiency; and Hydraulic Power Steering (HPS), a traditional system using hydraulic pressure for steering assistance. Each type serves different vehicle categories and consumer preferences, impacting performance, energy consumption, and overall driving experience.</p></p>
