<p><p>The Optical Display Module market comprises various types, including the Prism Scheme, which utilizes prisms to manipulate light for improved viewing angles. The Curved Mirror (Worm Eye) Solution employs curved mirrors to enhance image perception, offering a wide field of view. The Birdbath Scheme integrates a reflective surface to direct light efficiently, while the Optical Waveguide Solution uses waveguide technology for compact and lightweight designs. Each type addresses specific challenges in display technology, enhancing visual experiences across applications.</p></p>
