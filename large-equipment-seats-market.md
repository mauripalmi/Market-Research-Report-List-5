<p><p>The Large Equipment Seats market primarily features two types: mechanical suspension seats and air suspension seats. Mechanical suspension seats utilize a spring system to absorb shocks and vibrations, offering a cost-effective and durable option for operators. In contrast, air suspension seats use an air-filled cushion that adjusts to the operator's weight, providing superior comfort and customizable support. Both types are designed to enhance operator comfort and productivity in heavy machinery and equipment, addressing the demands of various industrial applications.</p></p>
