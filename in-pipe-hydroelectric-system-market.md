<p><p>In-Pipe Hydroelectric System market includes two main types: Internal System and External System. The Internal System is integrated within the existing piping system, utilizing the flow of water to generate electricity. On the other hand, the External System involves installing a separate turbine outside the piping system to capture the flowing water and convert it into energy. Both systems offer sustainable energy solutions by harnessing the power of water flow within pipelines for various applications.</p></p>
