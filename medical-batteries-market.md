<p><p>The medical batteries market encompasses various types, each with distinct characteristics. Lithium-ion (Li-ion) batteries offer high energy density and longer life, making them ideal for portable medical devices. Nickel cadmium (Ni-Cd) batteries are known for their durability and ability to perform well in extreme temperatures but suffer from memory effect. Nickel metal hydride (NiMH) batteries provide higher capacity than Ni-Cd and are more environmentally friendly. Alkaline-manganese batteries are cost-effective and commonly used in low-drain medical applications due to their reliability and availability.</p></p>
