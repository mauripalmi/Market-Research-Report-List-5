<p><strong>The Laboratory Hematology Analyzers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sheath Flow Hematology Analyzers</li><li>Laser Hematology Analyzers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Laboratory hematology analyzers are essential for analyzing blood components. Sheath flow hematology analyzers utilize a sheath fluid to focus blood cells as they pass through a laser detection area, enabling precise counting and sizing. In contrast, laser hematology analyzers employ laser light scatter to evaluate cellular characteristics, providing detailed information on cell morphology and populations. Both types are critical in clinical diagnostics for assessing hematological disorders, ensuring accurate patient management and treatment decisions.</p></p>
