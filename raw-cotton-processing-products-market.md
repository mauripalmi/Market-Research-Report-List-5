<p><p>The raw cotton processing products market includes three primary types: cotton fiber, cotton linters, and cottonseed. Cotton fiber is the primary product used in textiles, known for its softness and breathability. Cotton linters, the short fibers left after ginning, are used in paper, plastics, and other industrial applications. Cottonseed, the byproduct of cotton processing, serves as a source for oil, animal feed, and various industrial products. Each type plays a vital role in diverse industries, enhancing economic value.</p></p>
