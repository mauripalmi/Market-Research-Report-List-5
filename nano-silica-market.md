<p><strong>The Nano Silica Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>P type</li><li>S type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nano silica market is typically categorized into two types: P-type (precipitated) and S-type (fumed). P-type nano silica is produced through a chemical precipitation process, resulting in high purity and uniform particle size, making it ideal for applications in coatings, rubber, and composites. S-type nano silica, on the other hand, is generated via a vapor-phase process, providing high surface area and versatility, often used in pharmaceuticals, electronics, and as reinforcing agents. Both types cater to distinct industry needs.</p></p>
