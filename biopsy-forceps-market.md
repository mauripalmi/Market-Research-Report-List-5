<p><p>The biopsy forceps market is primarily categorized into rigid and flexible biopsy forceps. Rigid biopsy forceps are designed for precise tissue sampling in procedures like endoscopy, providing stability and control during biopsies of accessible lesions. Conversely, flexible biopsy forceps are engineered for navigating through curved anatomy, making them suitable for sampling tissues in hard-to-reach areas. Both types are essential in diagnostic and therapeutic applications, facilitating accurate histological analysis while minimizing patient discomfort.</p></p>
