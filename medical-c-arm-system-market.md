<p><strong>The Medical C Arm System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CCD Mobile C-arm</li><li>FPD Mobile C-arm</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Medical C-Arm System market is primarily categorized into two types: CCD Mobile C-arms and FPD Mobile C-arms. CCD Mobile C-arms use Charge-Coupled Device technology for imaging, providing clear details but with limited image resolution compared to FPD systems. FPD Mobile C-arms, on the other hand, utilize Flat Panel Detector technology, offering superior image quality, dynamic range, and lower radiation exposure. Both types enhance the capabilities of surgical and diagnostic procedures, but they cater to different clinical needs and preferences.</p></p>
