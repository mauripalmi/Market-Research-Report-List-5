<p><strong>The Tape for Wafer Dicing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UV</li><li>Non-UV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tape for wafer dicing market is categorized into UV and non-UV types, based on the curing method used. UV tape employs ultraviolet light to cure the adhesive, providing precise adhesion and easy removal without residue, making it ideal for sensitive applications. Non-UV tape uses heat or pressure for curing, offering robust performance in various environments, though it may leave residues upon removal. Both types serve crucial roles in semiconductor manufacturing, ensuring efficient wafer processing and integrity during dicing.</p></p>
