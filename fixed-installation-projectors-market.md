<p><p>The fixed-installation projectors market can be categorized into three main types: foothold, wall-hanging, and roof-hanging. Foothold projectors are placed on the ground, offering flexibility for various settings. Wall-hanging projectors are mounted on vertical surfaces, ideal for permanent installations in classrooms or conference rooms. Roof-hanging projectors are suspended from ceilings, optimizing space while providing an unobstructed display view. Each type addresses specific installation needs and preferences, enhancing the overall viewing experience in diverse environments.</p></p>
