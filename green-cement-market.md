<p><p>The green cement market consists of several types, each contributing to sustainable construction practices. Slag-based cement, made from steel industry byproducts, offers durability and lower carbon emissions. Recycled aggregates incorporate reclaimed materials, minimizing the need for virgin resources and landfill impact. Collectively, these options support environmental sustainability in the industry.</p></p>
