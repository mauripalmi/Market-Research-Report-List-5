<p><p>The MAG welding torch market is categorized into two main types: air-cooled and water-cooled torches. Air-cooled torches utilize ambient air to dissipate heat, making them lightweight and portable, ideal for short-duration welding tasks. In contrast, water-cooled torches use a water circulation system to manage heat, allowing for prolonged use in high-intensity applications. This distinction affects performance, cooling efficiency, and the range of suitable welding tasks, influencing user preference based on specific project requirements and operating conditions.</p></p>
