<p><p>The fluorocarbon gas market comprises several key types, each with distinct applications. Hexafluorobutadiene (C4F6) is leveraged in specialty polymers, and octafluorocyclobutane (C4F8) is essential in advanced manufacturing techniques. Tetrafluoromethane (CHF3) is employed in etching and cleaning processes. Other fluorocarbon gases cater to niche applications across various industries.</p></p>
