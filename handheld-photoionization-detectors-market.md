<p><p>The handheld photoionization detectors (PID) market consists of two main types: single photoionization detectors and multi photoionization detectors. Single photoionization detectors utilize a single ionization source to identify and measure specific volatile organic compounds (VOCs) in the environment. In contrast, multi photoionization detectors employ multiple ionization sources, allowing simultaneous detection of various compounds, enhancing sensitivity and breadth of analysis. Both types are crucial for applications in environmental monitoring, industrial safety, and emergency response, ensuring effective detection of hazardous substances.</p></p>
