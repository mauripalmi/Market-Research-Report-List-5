<p><p>Refrigerant dehumidifiers are essential for controlling humidity levels in various settings. Tower type dehumidifiers utilize a vertical design and often feature multiple layers of cooling coils, maximizing air contact for efficient moisture removal. Rotor type dehumidifiers, on the other hand, employ a rotating desiccant wheel that adsorbs moisture from the air, subsequently releasing it during a regeneration phase. Both types serve distinct applications, providing effective solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial humidity control by enhancing indoor air quality.</p></p>
