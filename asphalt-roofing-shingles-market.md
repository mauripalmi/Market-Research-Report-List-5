<p><p>The asphalt roofing shingles market is primarily divided into two main types: laminated shingles and three-tab shingles. Laminated shingles, also known as architectural shingles, feature a multi-layer design that provides a dimensional appearance and enhanced durability, making them popular for residential applications. In contrast, three-tab shingles have a flatter, simpler design with a uniform appearance, usually at a lower cost, and are often used for budget-conscious projects. Both types offer effective weather protection and aesthetic appeal.</p></p>
