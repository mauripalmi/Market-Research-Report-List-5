<p><strong>The Sedan Speed Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Sedan Speed Sensor market is categorized into Active and Passive types. Active speed sensors generate their own signal using power, offering high accuracy and reliability, often used in advanced systems like ABS and traction control. Passive speed sensors, on the other hand, rely on external magnetic fields to generate signals, making them simpler and cost-effective but less precise. Both types are integral to vehicle speed monitoring, with active sensors dominating modern, high-performance sedans and passive sensors in older or budget models.</p></p>
