<p><p>Guaranteed Asset Protection (GAP) Insurance comes in various market types. Finance GAP Insurance covers the gap between the vehicle's value and the remaining loan balance after a total loss. Return-to-invoice GAP Insurance refunds the original purchase price minus any insurance payout. Vehicle Replacement GAP Insurance provides a replacement vehicle of similar make and model. Return-to-value GAP Insurance accounts for depreciation, ensuring the policyholder receives the vehicle's original value. Other market types may include specialized policies tailored to specific needs.</p></p>
