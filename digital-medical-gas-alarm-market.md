<p><p>The Digital Medical Gas Alarm market consists of three primary types: Master Alarms, Area Alarms, and Local Alarms. Master Alarms serve as centralized monitoring systems, alerting medical staff to issues across multiple areas. Area Alarms are designed for specific zones within healthcare facilities, providing localized alerts about gas supply levels or malfunctions. Local Alarms operate at the point of use, incorporating immediate notifications for individual equipment, ensuring timely responses to potential hazards in patient care environments.</p></p>
