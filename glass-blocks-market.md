<p><p>The glass blocks market comprises two main types: solid glass blocks and hollow glass blocks. Solid glass blocks are robust and often used for structural purposes, providing excellent insulation and soundproofing. They are commonly used in architectural applications, such as walls and facades. Both types offer aesthetic appeal and practical benefits in various construction projects.</p></p>
