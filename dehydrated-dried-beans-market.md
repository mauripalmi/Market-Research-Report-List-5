<p><p>The dehydrated dried beans market includes two primary product types: dehydrated green bean granules and dehydrated green bean powder. Dehydrated green bean granules are coarsely processed, retaining some texture for use in soups, stews, and as a seasoning or side dish. In contrast, dehydrated green bean powder is finely ground, making it ideal for incorporation into smoothies, sauces, and baked goods, offering convenience and nutritional benefits. Both forms provide long shelf life and preserve the beans' flavor and nutrients.</p></p>
