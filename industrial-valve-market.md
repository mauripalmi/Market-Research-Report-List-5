<p><p>The industrial valve market consists of various types, each serving specific functions. Gate valves provide straight-line flow with minimal pressure drop, ideal for on/off control. Globe valves are designed for throttling and flow regulation. Ball valves offer quick shut-off capabilities with low resistance, suitable for high-flow applications. Plug valves enable easy flow control with a rotating plug mechanism. Buffer valves manage pressure within systems, while other types encompass specialized designs like check and butterfly valves, addressing diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
