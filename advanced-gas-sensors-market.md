<p><p>The advanced gas sensors market encompasses various technologies, primarily NDIR (Non-Dispersive Infrared) sensors and chemical sensors. NDIR sensors measure gas concentrations by detecting infrared light absorption, making them highly accurate for measuring gases like CO2 and CH4. In contrast, chemical sensors rely on specific chemical reactions or changes in properties to identify and quantify gases. Both types are integral to applications in environmental monitoring, industrial safety, and automotive systems, driving advancements in accuracy, sensitivity, and reliability.</p></p>
