<p><p>The Metal & Glass Coatings market is divided into three main types: solventborne, waterborne, and UV coatings. Solventborne coatings are solvent-based, offering strong adhesion and durability, suitable for various industrial applications. Waterborne coatings are eco-friendly, using water as a solvent, providing reduced VOC emissions and easier cleanup. UV coatings cure quickly under ultraviolet light, resulting in a hard finish that is resistant to scratches and chemicals, ideal for high-performance applications. Each type offers unique benefits tailored to specific needs.</p></p>
