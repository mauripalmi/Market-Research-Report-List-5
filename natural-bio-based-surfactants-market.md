<p><p>The natural bio-based surfactants market is segmented into four main types: Anionic, Nonionic, Cationic, and Amphoteric surfactants. Anionic surfactants carry a negative charge and are effective in cleaning and foaming applications. Nonionic surfactants, with no charge, provide good emulsification and stability in various formulations. Cationic surfactants have a positive charge, offering antimicrobial properties and often used in hair conditioners. Amphoteric surfactants can exhibit both positive and negative charges, making them versatile in different environments and applications.</p></p>
