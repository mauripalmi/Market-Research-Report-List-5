<p><ul><li>Intrinsic Self-healing Material</li><li>Ambush Self-healing Material</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Self-healing composites are classified into intrinsic and ambush self-healing materials. Intrinsic self-healing materials possess innate properties that enable them to autonomously repair damage upon exposure to certain stimuli, such as heat or moisture, making them ideal for applications requiring durability. In contrast, ambush self-healing materials incorporate pre-loaded healing agents that activate upon damage, allowing for localized repair. Both types enhance the longevity and reliability of composite structures in various industries, including aerospace, automotive, and construction.</p></p>
