<p><p>Next Generation Packaging encompasses three key types: Active Packaging, Intelligent Packaging, and Modified Atmosphere Packaging. Active Packaging involves incorporating substances that actively interact with food to extend shelf life and maintain quality. Intelligent Packaging features sensors or indicators that provide real-time information about the product's condition, enhancing safety and transparency. Modified Atmosphere Packaging alters the gaseous environment surrounding the product to slow spoilage and preserve freshness, making it ideal for perishable goods. Together, these innovations enhance food quality and safety.</p></p>
