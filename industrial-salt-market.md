<p><p>The industrial salt market comprises various types, including sea salt, well and rock salt, and lake salt. Well and rock salt, derived from underground deposits, is primarily used in industrial applications, including de-icing and water treatment. Lake salt, obtained from saline lake sources, is also used in various applications, including chemical production. Each type serves distinct industrial needs, driven by availability and specific use cases.</p></p>
