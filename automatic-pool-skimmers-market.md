<p><p>The automatic pool skimmer market consists of three main types: crawler drive, wheel drive, and solar-powered models. Crawler drive skimmers use a continuous track mechanism for better maneuverability across various surfaces, effectively tackling debris in uneven pools. Wheel drive models utilize wheels for swift movement and ease of navigation, ideal for flat surfaces. Solar-powered skimmers harness renewable energy for efficient operation, providing environmentally friendly pool cleaning solutions while reducing energy costs and reliance on electricity.</p></p>
