<p><p>The dielectric paste market is categorized into two main types: curing paste and sintered paste. Curing paste is used in applications requiring heat to harden or set, providing excellent electrical insulation and stability for electronic components. Sintered paste, on the other hand, is utilized in applications where materials are fused through heat without melting, enhancing conductivity and structural integrity in devices. Both types are essential in improving performance and reliability in various electronic and electrical systems.</p></p>
