<p><p>Non-absorbable sutures are surgical threads that do not dissolve or assimilate into the body over time, making them ideal for long-term wound support. These sutures are commonly made from materials such as silk, nylon, polyester, and polypropylene, and are used in a variety of surgical settings, including orthopedic, cardiac, and plastic surgeries. They provide durability and tensile strength, ensuring that the wound remains securely closed until sufficient healing occurs.</p><p>The Non Absorbable Sutures Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.20% during the forecast period. Factors contributing to this growth include the increasing prevalence of chronic diseases requiring surgical interventions, a rising geriatric population, and advancements in surgical techniques. Furthermore, the growing trend of minimally invasive surgeries is driving demand for specialized non-absorbable sutures that can effectively secure tissues in these procedures.</p><p>In addition, innovations in suture materials, such as antibacterial coatings and bioengineered products, are enhancing the market's appeal. Emerging markets are also presenting new opportunities due to the expanding healthcare infrastructure and increasing awareness about surgical procedures and post-operative care. Overall, the non-absorbable sutures market is poised for significant advancement in the coming years.</p></p>
