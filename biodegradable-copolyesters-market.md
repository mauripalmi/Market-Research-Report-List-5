<p><p>The biodegradable copolyesters market primarily includes Polybutylene Succinate (PBS) and Polyhydroxyalkanoates (PHA). PBS is a versatile, biodegradable polymer derived from renewable resources, ideal for packaging and agricultural applications, due to its good thermal and mechanical properties. PHA, also produced from biological materials, offers superior biodegradability and adaptability across various environmental conditions, making it suitable for medical and food packaging applications. Both materials are gaining traction as sustainable alternatives to conventional plastics in response to increasing environmental concerns.</p></p>
