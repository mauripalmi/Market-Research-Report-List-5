<p><p>Anti-friction bearing market can be categorized into three main types: ball bearings, roller bearings, and other bearings. Ball bearings use balls to reduce friction between moving parts, while roller bearings use cylindrical rollers. Other types of bearings include needle bearings, thrust bearings, and spherical bearings, each designed for specific applications. Ball bearings are commonly used in machinery and equipment where smooth and precise movement is required, while roller bearings are preferred for heavy-duty applications where high radial loads are present.</p></p>
