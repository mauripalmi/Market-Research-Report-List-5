<p><ul><li>Chemiluminescence Method</li><li>Immunoturbidimetry</li><li>Latex Turbidimetric Method</li><li>Colorimetry</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ferritin Determination Kit market is segmented into various methods for measuring ferritin levels, including Chemiluminescence, Immunoturbidimetry, Latex Turbidimetric, and Colorimetry. Chemiluminescence method utilizes light emission for sensitive detection, while Immunoturbidimetry relies on the cloudiness caused by antigen-antibody reactions. The Latex Turbidimetric Method employs latex particles to enhance turbidity, promoting accurate readings. Lastly, Colorimetry measures color intensity changes, providing a straightforward, cost-effective approach. Each method offers unique advantages, catering to diverse laboratory needs and preferences.</p></p>
