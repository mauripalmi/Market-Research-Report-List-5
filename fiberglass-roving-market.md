<p><strong>The Fiberglass Roving Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-end Roving</li><li>Multi-end Roving</li><li>Chopped Roving</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiberglass roving market consists of three main types: single-end roving, multi-end roving, and chopped roving. Single-end roving features individual strands of fiberglass, ideal for applications requiring high strength in thin layers, such as composites. Multi-end roving combines multiple strands for enhanced strength and durability, suitable for larger structures like boats and automotive parts. Chopped roving, on the other hand, consists of short strands used in various applications, including spray-up processes and reinforced plastics, providing versatility in manufacturing.</p></p>
