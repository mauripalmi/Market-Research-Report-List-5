<p><strong>The Spatial Light Modulator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reflective SLM</li><li>Transmissive SLM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Spatial Light Modulator (SLM) market is primarily divided into reflective and transmissive types. Reflective SLMs manipulate light by reflecting it off a surface, making them suitable for applications like projectors and displays where high efficiency and brightness are essential. In contrast, transmissive SLMs work by allowing light to pass through them, ideal for applications such as optical communication and holography, where precise control over light paths is crucial. Both types serve diverse industries, enhancing imaging and display technologies.</p></p>
