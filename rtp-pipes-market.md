<p><p>RTP (Reinforced Thermoplastic) pipes are categorized into Non-metallic and Metallic RTP markets based on their reinforcement materials. Non-metallic RTP pipes use high-strength synthetic fibers like aramid or glass for reinforcement, offering lightweight, corrosion-resistant solutions ideal for water, gas, and chemical transport. Metallic RTP pipes incorporate steel wires or strips, providing higher pressure resistance and durability, suitable for oil, gas, and industrial applications. Both types combine flexibility, strength, and longevity, catering to diverse infrastructure needs while addressing specific operational and environmental challenges.</p></p>
