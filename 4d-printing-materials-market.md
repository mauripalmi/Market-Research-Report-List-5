<p><strong>The 4D Printing Materials Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shape Memory Polymer (SMP)</li><li>Liquid Crystal Elastomer (LCE)</li><li>Shape Memory Alloy (SMA)</li><li>Hydrogel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 4D printing materials market includes several innovative types: </p><p>1. **Shape Memory Polymer (SMP)**—these materials can transition between two shapes upon stimuli, offering flexibility in design.</p><p>2. **Liquid Crystal Elastomer (LCE)**—these combine elasticity with liquid crystal properties, enabling complex movements and shapes in response to temperature changes.</p><p>3. **Shape Memory Alloy (SMA)**—metal alloys that return to a predetermined shape when heated, ideal for applications requiring mechanical actuation.</p><p>4. **Hydrogel**—water-absorbing polymers that change properties and shape in response to environmental stimuli, useful in biomedical applications.</p></p>
