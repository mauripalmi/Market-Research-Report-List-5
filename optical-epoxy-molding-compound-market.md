<p><ul><li>Solid Optical EMC</li><li>Liquid Optical EMC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Optical Epoxy Molding Compound (EMC) market is primarily divided into two types: Solid Optical EMC and Liquid Optical EMC. Solid Optical EMC is a pre-mixed, resin-based material that provides excellent thermal stability and optical clarity, typically used in high-performance optical applications. In contrast, Liquid Optical EMC is a low-viscosity material that can easily fill intricate mold designs, ensuring precise reproduction of complex shapes. Both types are essential for manufacturing optical components like lenses, LEDs, and integrated circuits.</p></p>
