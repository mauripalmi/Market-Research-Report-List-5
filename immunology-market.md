<p><p>The immunology market can be divided into two main types - immuno boosters and immunosuppressants. Immuno boosters are products that help enhance the immune system's response to fight off infections and diseases. On the other hand, immunosuppressants are medications that suppress the immune system, commonly used in conditions where the immune system is overactive or attacking healthy cells. Both markets play a crucial role in managing various immune-related conditions and diseases by either enhancing or suppressing the immune response.</p></p>
