<p><p>Camelina sativa oil is derived through two primary extraction methods: hexane extraction and cold pressing. Hexane-extracted oil (HEO) involves using hexane as a solvent to efficiently extract oil, resulting in a higher yield and a more refined product. In contrast, cold-pressed oil (CPO) is obtained by mechanically pressing the seeds without heat, preserving more nutrients and flavor. Both methods serve distinct markets, appealing to consumers seeking either refined or naturally nutritious oil options for culinary and industrial uses.</p></p>
