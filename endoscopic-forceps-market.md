<p><strong>The Endoscopic Forceps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monopolar Endoscopic Forceps</li><li>Bipolar Endoscopic Forceps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Endoscopic Forceps market is segmented into two main types: Monopolar Endoscopic Forceps and Bipolar Endoscopic Forceps. Monopolar Endoscopic Forceps utilize a single electrode for cutting and coagulation, allowing for precise tissue manipulation during procedures. In contrast, Bipolar Endoscopic Forceps employ two electrodes for localized energy application, minimizing unintended thermal damage to surrounding tissues. Each type offers distinct advantages, catering to the varying needs of surgical techniques and procedures in minimally invasive surgeries, thus driving market growth.</p></p>
