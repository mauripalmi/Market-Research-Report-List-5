<p><p>Liquid supersonic flowmeters are sophisticated instruments used to measure fluid flow rates at supersonic speeds. The market is divided into two main types: non-contact supersonic flowmeters and contact supersonic flowmeters. Non-contact flowmeters utilize advanced technologies to measure flow without physical interaction with the fluid, enhancing accuracy and reducing wear. In contrast, contact flowmeters involve direct interaction with the liquid, providing precise measurements but may be subject to wear over time due to friction and exposure to the medium.</p></p>
