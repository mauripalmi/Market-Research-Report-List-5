<p><p>The superalloys market is primarily categorized into three types: nickel-based, nickel-iron based, and cobalt-based superalloys. Nickel-iron based superalloys offer a balance of strength and ductility, suitable for various industrial applications. Cobalt-based superalloys are known for their excellent corrosion resistance and are often used in high-stress environments like gas turbines and chemical processing. Together, they cater to diverse industries requiring advanced performance materials.</p></p>
