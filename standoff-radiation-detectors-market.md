<p><p>The standoff radiation detectors market encompasses three primary types: gamma detection, neutron detection, and source localization. Gamma detection identifies gamma radiation emitted from radioactive materials, crucial for security and environmental monitoring. Neutron detection targets neutron emissions, often used in nuclear facilities and for identifying illicit materials. Source localization technologies enable the pinpointing of radiation sources, enhancing response strategies in safety and security efforts. Collectively, these sensing technologies play a vital role in threat assessment and radiation safety management.</p></p>
