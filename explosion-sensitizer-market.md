<p><ul><li>Physical Sensitizer</li><li>Chemosensitizers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The explosion sensitizer market comprises two main types: physical sensitizers and chemosensitizers. Physical sensitizers enhance the sensitivity of explosives to external stimuli such as heat or pressure, thereby increasing their responsiveness. Chemosensitizers, on the other hand, modify the chemical dynamics of explosive materials, altering their reaction rates and stability. Both types play crucial roles in improving safety and efficiency in various industries, including mining, construction, and demolition, by optimizing explosive performance and minimizing unintended detonations.</p></p>
