<p><strong>The Acrylic Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermosetting Acrylic Resin</li><li>Thermoplastic Acrylic Resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The acrylic resin market is primarily divided into two types: thermosetting and thermoplastic acrylic resins. Thermosetting acrylic resins, once cured, retain their shape permanently and are known for their durability and resistance to heat, making them ideal for applications in automotive and construction industries. In contrast, thermoplastic acrylic resins can be reshaped when heated, offering flexibility and easy processing. They are commonly used in coatings, adhesives, and signage, benefiting from their clarity and UV resistance.</p></p>
