<p><p>The medical-grade N95 protective mask market encompasses primarily two types: flat-fold and cup type. They often feature adjustable straps for comfort and secure sealing, making them popular for healthcare settings. Cup-type masks, on the other hand, maintain a rigid shape, allowing for increased durability and better airflow. Both types effectively filter out airborne particles, ensuring high protection levels for healthcare professionals and patients.</p></p>
