<p><ul><li>Leading Trailing Shoe Brake</li><li>Dual Two Trailing Shoe Brake</li><li>Dual Two Leading Shoe Brake</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Drum brake markets are categorized based on the arrangement of the brake shoes within the drum. Leading trailing shoe brake features one leading and one trailing shoe, with the leading shoe facing the direction of rotation. Dual two trailing shoe brake has two trailing shoes, while dual two leading shoe brake has two leading shoes. Each type offers different advantages in terms of braking performance, heat dissipation, and wear characteristics, catering to various vehicle applications and performance requirements.</p></p>
