<p><p>Church Management Software (CMS) is categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based CMS allows churches to access software via the internet, offering flexibility, automatic updates, and reduced IT costs. This model supports remote access and collaboration among staff and volunteers. On-premise CMS, on the other hand, requires local installation on church servers, providing more control over data and customization but demanding greater upfront investment and maintenance. Each type serves different needs and preferences within the church community.</p></p>
