<p><strong>The Drying Systems for Softgels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tumbler Dryer & Drying Tunnels</li><li>In-Line Drying</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Drying systems for softgels are essential for achieving optimal moisture content and shelf stability. Tumbler dryers utilize a rotating drum to evenly distribute heat and airflow, ensuring uniform drying of softgel products. Drying tunnels, on the other hand, consist of a continuous conveyor system that moves softgels through a controlled environment where hot air circulates, promoting efficient moisture removal. In-line drying systems integrate drying within the production process, streamlining operations and enhancing overall efficiency for softgel manufacturing.</p></p>
