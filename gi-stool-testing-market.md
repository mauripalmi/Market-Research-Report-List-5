<p><p>GI stool testing market includes a variety of tests such as microscopy tests, which involve examining stool samples under a microscope to identify parasites or abnormal cells. Ova & parasites test specifically looks for parasites in the stool. Occult blood test detects blood in the stool which can indicate gastrointestinal bleeding. Stool biomarkers tests analyze specific proteins or DNA markers in the stool to diagnose conditions like inflammatory bowel disease. Other tests in this market include those for detecting infections, malabsorption, and digestive enzyme deficiencies.</p></p>
