<p><p>The non-biodegradable recycled thermoplastics market encompasses various product forms, including flakes, pellets, and granulates. Flakes are small, irregular pieces created from processed plastic waste, providing versatility for various applications. Pellets are uniform, small plastic grains formed from flakes, ideal for injection or extrusion molding. Granulates are larger particles, often used in a broader range of industries. Together, these forms enable efficient recycling and repurposing of plastics, contributing to sustainability efforts while meeting diverse manufacturing needs.</p></p>
