<p><p>The needle-free drug injection systems market encompasses several innovative technologies. Spring-based needle-free injectors rely on mechanical springs to generate pressure, pushing the drug into the body. Laser-powered needle-free injectors use laser technology to create micro-channels in the skin for delivering drugs. Vibration-based needle-free injectors leverage mechanical vibrations to facilitate drug entry, enhancing patient comfort and compliance in various therapeutic applications.</p></p>
