<p><strong>The Programmable PWM Controllers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Current Mode PWM Controllers</li><li>Voltage Mode PWM Controllers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Programmable PWM controllers are essential in power management applications, allowing for efficient control of voltage and current levels. Current mode PWM controllers regulate output by adjusting the duty cycle based on the inductor current, providing fast response and inherent overcurrent protection. In contrast, voltage mode PWM controllers focus on maintaining a desired output voltage by modulating the duty cycle according to the output voltage feedback. These types serve various applications, including power supplies, motor drives, and renewable energy systems, catering to different performance and efficiency requirements.</p></p>
