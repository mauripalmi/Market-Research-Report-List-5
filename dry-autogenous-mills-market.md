<p><p>Dry Autogenous Mills are grinding mills that operate without the addition of water or other liquids, utilizing the ore itself as a grinding medium. These mills are particularly effective in processing hard and coarse materials, making them integral in sectors such as mining and minerals processing. The primary advantage of dry autogenous milling is its energy efficiency and lower operational costs, as it eliminates the need for water handling and waste disposal.</p><p>The Dry Autogenous Mills Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand for mineral-based products and the rising need for efficient grinding technologies.
