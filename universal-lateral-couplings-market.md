<p><p>The Universal Lateral Couplings market includes two main types: Clamp Universal Lateral Couplings and Screw Universal Lateral Couplings. Clamp Universal Lateral Couplings utilize a clamping mechanism to securely connect pipes or shafts, allowing for easy installation and disassembly. They are often preferred for applications requiring frequent maintenance. In contrast, Screw Universal Lateral Couplings use threaded connections to provide a strong, stable coupling, suitable for high-torque applications. Both types are essential for efficient fluid and power transmission in various industrial sectors.</p></p>
