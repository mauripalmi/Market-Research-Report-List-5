<p><p>The GaAs RF Switching ICs market is primarily categorized into two types: SMD/SMT (Surface-Mount Device/Surface-Mount Technology) and Through Hole. SMD/SMT devices are compact and suitable for automated assembly processes, offering improved performance and reduced footprint, making them ideal for modern high-density applications. In contrast, Through Hole components are larger and often provide enhanced durability and thermal performance, commonly used in applications requiring higher power handling or in rugged environments. Both types serve specific needs within the RF switching applications.</p></p>
