<p><p>Facade market types can be broadly categorized into Non-Ventilated Facades, Ventilated Facades, and Others. Non-Ventilated Facades are typically constructed without an air gap between the cladding and the building structure. Ventilated Facades include a gap for air circulation between the cladding and the building, allowing for better insulation and moisture management. Other types of facades may include hybrid systems or specialized designs that do not fit into the traditional categories of facade construction. Each type offers unique benefits and considerations based on the specific needs of the building.</p></p>
