<p><ul><li>Manual Control</li><li>Electronic Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Air Suspension Controller market includes two primary types: Manual Control and Electronic Control. Manual Control systems require user intervention to adjust suspension settings, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness but limited precision. Electronic Control systems, on the other hand, use sensors and automated algorithms to dynamically adjust suspension based on driving conditions, providing enhanced comfort, stability, and performance. While manual systems are suited for basic applications, electronic systems cater to advanced automotive needs, emphasizing convenience and adaptability. Both types serve distinct market segments based on functionality and user requirements.</p></p>
