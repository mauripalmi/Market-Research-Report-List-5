<p><p>The electric energy metering box market is divided into two primary types: single-phase and three-phase meters. Single-phase metering boxes (SMC) are typically used in residential settings to measure lower electricity consumption, making them ideal for homes and small businesses. In contrast, three-phase metering boxes (PC) are designed for industrial and commercial applications that require higher power capacity, efficiently measuring larger energy loads. Both types play a crucial role in energy management and billing accuracy.</p></p>
