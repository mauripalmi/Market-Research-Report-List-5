<p><p>Chromium target markets include two primary types: plane targets and rotating targets. Plane targets are flat, uniform surfaces used in various applications like thin-film deposition, providing consistent material distribution. In contrast, rotating targets are cylindrical, allowing for enhanced material utilization and efficiency during the sputtering process. The rotating mechanism reduces heat concentration and prolongs target life while ensuring uniform coating across surfaces. Together, these target types cater to diverse industrial and research needs in semiconductor, optics, and coatings industries.</p></p>
