<p><p>The wireless smart lighting control market features several technologies, each offering unique advantages. Zigbee operates on low power, ideal for mesh networks, enabling extensive device interconnectivity. Wi-Fi provides high-speed connectivity and broad compatibility but may consume more energy. Bluetooth is user-friendly and suitable for short-range applications, while Z-Wave focuses on home automation with reliable, low-latency communication. Enocean offers energy harvesting solutions, eliminating the need for batteries. Together, these technologies cater to diverse consumer preferences and applications in smart lighting.</p></p>
