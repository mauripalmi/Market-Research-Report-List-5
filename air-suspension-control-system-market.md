<p><p>The Air Suspension Control System market includes three main types: Capsule, Membrane, and Compound systems. Capsule systems use air-filled rubber capsules to provide cushioning, offering compact design and durability. Membrane systems utilize flexible rubber membranes to regulate air pressure, ensuring smooth ride quality and adaptability. Compound systems combine elements of both, integrating capsules and membranes for enhanced performance, stability, and load-bearing capacity. These systems cater to diverse automotive and industrial applications, balancing comfort, efficiency, and durability.</p></p>
