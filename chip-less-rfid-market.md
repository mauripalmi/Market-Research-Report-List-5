<p><p>Chip-less RFID technology includes Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) and Time Domain Reflectometry (TFRF) or Time Frequency Transformation Coding (TFTC) markets. SAW technology utilizes acoustic waves to store and transmit data, making it durable and reliable for applications in harsh environments. TFTC technology represents a new generation of chip-less RFID that offers enhanced security and flexibility in data storage and transmission. Both SAW and TFTC markets provide different solutions for various industries including retail, healthcare, logistics, and automotive sectors.</p></p>
