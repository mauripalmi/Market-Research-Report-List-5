<p><p>The high power resistors market is segmented into three main types: chassis mounted, surface mounted, and through-hole mounted. Chassis mounted resistors are designed for robust applications requiring direct attachment to a chassis for heat dissipation. Surface mounted resistors are compact and ideal for modern circuit boards where space is limited, allowing for automated assembly processes. Through-hole mounted resistors offer strong mechanical support and are commonly used in traditional applications where durability and high power handling are essential.</p></p>
