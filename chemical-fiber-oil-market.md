<p><p>The chemical fiber oil market primarily consists of three types: Drawn Textured Yarn (DTY), Fully Drawn Yarn (FDY), and Partially Oriented Yarn (POY). DTY is known for its elasticity and softness, often used in textiles requiring stretch. FDY offers high strength and is suitable for applications needing durability. POY, with its semi-oriented structure, serves as a base for further processing into DTY or FDY. The demand for these yarns drives the market, influencing the chemical fiber oil production process.</p></p>
