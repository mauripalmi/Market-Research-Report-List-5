<p><p>The microduct cable market consists of various types, primarily Thick Walled Ducts (TWD) and Tight Protected Ducts (TPD). TWD is designed for high durability, ideal for harsh environments, providing robust protection and thermal stability for fiber optic cables. In contrast, TPD offers a more flexible solution, ensuring protection while accommodating tight spaces, making it suitable for urban deployments. Both types serve the growing demand for efficient, high-capacity fiber networks, enhancing communication infrastructure across various applications.</p></p>
