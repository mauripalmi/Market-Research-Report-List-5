<p><p>The blocked isocyanate market is segmented into water-based and solvent-based types. Water-based blocked isocyanates are environmentally friendly, offering low VOC emissions and are suitable for indoor applications, often used in coatings and adhesives. Solvent-based blocked isocyanates provide excellent performance in harsher conditions and are preferred for industrial applications due to their durability and quick curing properties. Both types serve as essential components in the formulation of polyurethane systems, enhancing flexibility, adhesion, and resistance to chemicals.</p></p>
