<p><p>The Miniature Zinc Die Castings market features several casting types, each with unique processes. Pressure Die Casting involves injecting molten zinc into molds under high pressure, ensuring precise details. Vacuum Die Casting eliminates air pockets by creating a vacuum, enhancing structural integrity. Squeeze Die Casting applies pressure to ensure uniform metal distribution, suitable for complex shapes. Semi-Solid Die Casting utilizes partially solidified metal for improved flow and reduced defects. Other methods may include gravity and low-pressure casting, catering to diverse applications.</p></p>
