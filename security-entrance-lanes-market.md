<p><p>The security entrance lanes market is primarily divided into two types: electric sensing lanes and optical sensing lanes. Electric sensing lanes use electronic components, such as RFID and biometrics, to monitor and control access, ensuring high security. In contrast, optical sensing lanes utilize advanced sensors and cameras to detect unauthorized entries through image recognition and movement tracking. Both types enhance security in commercial and public spaces, streamlining access control and improving surveillance while maintaining a seamless flow of authorized individuals.</p></p>
