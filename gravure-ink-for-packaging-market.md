<p><p>Gravure ink for packaging is categorized based on its solvent type, influencing application and performance. **Water-based gravure ink** is eco-friendly, ideal for flexible packaging. **Alcohol gravure ink** offers quick-drying properties, suitable for high-speed printing processes. **Benzene gravure ink**, while less commonly used due to health concerns, provides deep color and durability. **Gasoline gravure ink** features strong solvent properties but faces similar regulatory challenges. The **other** category encompasses specialized inks tailored for specific substrates or unique printing requirements.</p></p>
