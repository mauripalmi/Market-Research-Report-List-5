<p><p>The barley market includes several product types, each with distinct uses. Quick pearl barley is processed for quick cooking, making it convenient for meals. Barley grits are coarsely ground for dishes like polenta. Hulless barley is minimally processed, while hulled barley retains its bran and nutrients, appealing to health-conscious consumers.</p></p>
