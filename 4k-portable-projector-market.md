<p><p>The 4K portable projector market features three primary technologies: LCD, DLP, and LCoS. </p><p>LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) projectors utilize liquid crystals to produce vibrant images and are known for their brightness and color accuracy. </p><p>DLP (Digital Light Processing) projectors rely on micro-mirrors to create sharp images and are valued for their compact size and smooth motion handling.  </p><p>LCoS (Liquid Crystal on Silicon) projectors combine qualities of both, offering high contrast and detailed visuals, making them ideal for home theater use. Each type caters to diverse user preferences and applications.</p></p>
