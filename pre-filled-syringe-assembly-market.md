<p><p>The pre-filled syringe assembly market consists of various types of materials used for syringe construction. COP (Cyclic Olefin Polymer) offers excellent clarity and low moisture absorption, making it suitable for sensitive drugs. COC (Cyclic Olefin Copolymer) provides similar benefits but with enhanced gas barrier properties. PP (Polypropylene) is a cost-effective option, known for its chemical resistance and ease of manufacturing. Other materials may include glass and elastomers, catering to specific requirements in pharmaceuticals and biologics delivery systems.</p></p>
