Single Pocket systems are designed for straightforward applications, allowing for efficient vapor deposition in a single chamber. Rotary Pocket systems offer enhanced versatility and throughput by enabling multiple sources within a rotating configuration, ideal for larger-scale production. Linear Pocket systems provide linear configurations for continuous processing, optimizing space and improving uniformity across substrates. Each type caters to different manufacturing needs and efficiencies.</p></p>
