<p><p>The automotive suspension market consists of various types of suspension components, including coil springs, leaf springs, stabilizer bars, suspension arms, and others. Coil springs are helical springs that absorb shocks and maintain the height of the vehicle. Leaf springs are made of multiple layers of metal strips that support the weight of the vehicle. Stabilizer bars reduce body roll during cornering. Suspension arms connect the frame to the wheels, allowing for flexibility and movement. Other components include shock absorbers, struts, and air springs, all of which contribute to a smooth and stable ride.</p></p>
