<p><p>Translation Management Software (TMS) market is primarily divided into two types: On-Premise and Cloud-based solutions. On-Premise TMS requires installation and maintenance on the user's server, providing greater control and security. Cloud-based TMS, on the other hand, is hosted on the vendor's server and accessed via the internet, offering flexibility and scalability. While On-Premise solutions may be preferred by organizations with strict data security requirements, Cloud-based TMS is gaining popularity due to its cost-effectiveness and ease of implementation.</p></p>
