<p><p>The fluorescent pigments market is categorized mainly into thermoset and thermoplastic types, along with other forms like aqueous dispersions. Thermoset fluorescent pigments are durable and resistant to heat, making them ideal for coatings and plastics that require a stable color over time. In contrast, thermoplastic pigments are more flexible and suitable for applications that undergo reshaping or melting. Other variations, such as aqueous dispersions, offer environmentally friendly options, providing easy application in water-based systems without harmful solvents.</p></p>
