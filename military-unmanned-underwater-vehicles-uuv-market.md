<p><strong>The Military Unmanned Underwater Vehicles (UUV) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Remotely Operated Vehicle (ROV)</li><li>Autonomous Underwater Vehicle (AUV)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Military Unmanned Underwater Vehicles (UUV) market comprises primarily two types: Remotely Operated Vehicles (ROVs) and Autonomous Underwater Vehicles (AUVs). ROVs are tethered vehicles controlled from a surface vessel, used for tasks like surveillance, inspection, and underwater repairs. In contrast, AUVs operate independently, relying on pre-programmed missions for tasks such as oceanographic data collection and reconnaissance. Both UUV types are essential for modern naval operations, enhancing capabilities in surveillance, reconnaissance, and mine detection while minimizing risk to personnel.</p></p>
