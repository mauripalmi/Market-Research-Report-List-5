<p><p>Biological surgical sealants and adhesives are crucial in medical procedures, aiding in tissue bonding and wound closure. Fibrin-based sealants mimic the natural clotting process, promoting hemostasis and facilitating tissue repair. Collagen-based adhesives utilize collagen's inherent properties to enhance wound healing and provide structural support. Albumin-based sealants leverage albumin's adhesive qualities for effective tissue adherence, often used in vascular surgeries. Each type offers distinct advantages, catering to various surgical needs and enhancing patient outcomes.</p></p>
