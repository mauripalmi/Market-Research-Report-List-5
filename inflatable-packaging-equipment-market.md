Air pillow output, on the other hand, consists of smaller inflatable pouches that offer lightweight protection and are often used to fill voids in packages. Both types enhance product safety while optimizing shipping efficiency, reducing material usage, and minimizing environmental impact compared to traditional packaging methods.</p></p>
