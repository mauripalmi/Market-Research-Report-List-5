<p><strong>The Cooling Fan Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial Fans</li><li>Centrifugal Fans</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cooling fan market is primarily categorized into axial fans and centrifugal fans, with additional types classified as "others." Axial fans move air parallel to the fan's axis, making them suitable for applications requiring high airflow and low pressure, like cooling in electronics. Centrifugal fans, on the other hand, draw air in at an angle and expel it at a right angle, providing higher pressure for industrial ventilation. The "others" category includes specialized fans like mixed flow and blower fans, catering to niche applications.</p></p>
