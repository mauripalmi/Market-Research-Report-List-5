<p><p>Architecture glass curtain wall market types are categorized based on their frame support structure. Frame Support Curtain Walls are supported by aluminum or steel frames, while All-glass Curtain Walls utilize glass panels without visible frames. Point Support Curtain Walls are supported by discrete points, providing a seamless and minimalist look. Unit-typed Curtain Walls consist of pre-assembled units that are installed on the building facade. Each type offers different design possibilities and features, catering to various architectural needs and aesthetic preferences in the construction industry.</p></p>
