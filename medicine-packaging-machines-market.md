<p><p>Medicine packaging machines can be classified into two main types: automatic and semi-automatic. Automatic machines require minimal human intervention, as they can perform all packaging processes, such as filling, sealing, and labeling, automatically. On the other hand, semi-automatic machines require some level of human assistance, such as manually placing products into the machine or controlling certain aspects of the packaging process. Both types of machines are widely used in the pharmaceutical industry to increase efficiency and ensure accurate and consistent packaging of medicines.</p></p>
